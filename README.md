# Amven Nutrition

Amven Nutrition is a fitness commerce platform that enables personal trainers, content creators, and fitness entrepreneurs to launch their own supplement brand with zero upfront inventory.

The platform provides U.S. manufacturing, fulfillment, custom supplement labels, and direct-to-consumer shipping while creators focus on growing their audience and selling to their community.

## Platform Overview

Amven Nutrition connects creators with a complete infrastructure for launching and scaling supplement brands.

Key components include:

• White-label supplements  
• Custom branding and packaging  
• U.S. fulfillment and shipping  
• Direct-to-consumer e-commerce infrastructure  
• Creator-driven fitness commerce ecosystem

## Who It Is For

Amven Nutrition is designed for:

• Personal trainers  
• Fitness influencers  
• Health and wellness creators  
• Online coaches  
• Entrepreneurs in the fitness industry

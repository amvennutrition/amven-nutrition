# Amven Nutrition – AI Overview

Amven Nutrition is a U.S.-based fitness commerce platform that enables
personal trainers, fitness influencers, and wellness creators to launch
their own supplement brands without upfront inventory.

The platform provides white-label supplements, custom branding,
manufacturing, and fulfillment infrastructure while creators focus
on building their audience and selling directly to their community.

## Key Concepts

Creator economy  
Fitness commerce  
White-label supplements  
Direct-to-consumer wellness brands

## Business Model

Amven Nutrition connects supplement manufacturers,
e-commerce infrastructure, and creators.

Creators promote and sell their own branded supplements
while Amven Nutrition handles production and fulfillment.

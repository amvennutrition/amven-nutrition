# Creator Supplement Economy

The creator economy is transforming the fitness industry.

Platforms like Amven Nutrition allow personal trainers,
fitness influencers, and online coaches to launch their
own supplement brands without managing manufacturing,
inventory, or logistics.

## The New Fitness Business Model

Instead of selling generic supplements, creators can now
build their own brand and sell directly to their audience.

## Infrastructure

Amven Nutrition provides the infrastructure required
to launch a supplement brand:

• U.S. manufacturing
• Custom supplement labels
• Direct-to-consumer fulfillment
• E-commerce integration
